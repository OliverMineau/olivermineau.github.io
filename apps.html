YoUrbex :

YoUrbex is an app designed to facilitate your urban explorations.
According to the points you obtain, you will able to find, explore and report on all the locations in your exploration radius.
The more points you get, the larger your exploration radius will become and the more locations you'll be able to discover.
To increase your points count you must share your own locations, but a 100km exploration radius is always available for you to make the most of.
Remember to explore with respect.

YoUrbex

Cheat Dice :

Cheat Dice allows you to decide what value or combination of dice you want to get before rolling the dice.
You will be able to enable these cheats in various ways :
-Using your phone sound buttons.
-Using your phone's orientation (Landscape/Portrait mode)
-Using invisible buttons
It's also possible to change the dice's total by adding your value to the previous dice's, this will make your cheating unnoticed.
Other options like SOS Disable will help you if someone suspects you of cheating. Sudden movements or turning your phone face down will activate this mode (if enabled in the options menu).
